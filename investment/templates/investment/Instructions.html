In this experiment you will play an investment task. In the beginning, you get assigned either a low endowment of 100 Euro, or a high endwoment of 1000 Euro. Both amounts are equiprobable. You can then choose to invest an arbitrary amount of money into an investment option.


With a probability of 0.5 this investment will be successful. In this case your invested amount will be doubled. Otherwise, the amount invested is lost. In any case, you will also receive whatever is left of your endowment.
